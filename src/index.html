Why are prices different between boys and girls?

As a whole men have lower life expectency than women.

Men generally tend to lead much riskier lifestyles than women. All you have to do is look at automobile accident statistics and you will see that males have a much higher accident and fatality rate than women.

Young males especially also seem to engage on average in much riskier activities than women. Activities such as contact sports, many forms of racing activities such as auto and motorcycling racing, and other similar activities often result in higher mortality rates for men than women who tend on average to engage in less dangerous hobbies and sporting activities.

Finally, men tend to lead less healthier lifestyles and be employeed in riskier occupations.

What should I expect the process to be like?

Insuring new borns is a very simple non-invasive process. As an adult you can be subject to a battery of tests, procedures, blood
withdrawls, and even an EKG. We assure you, your child will rest easy during the process! See footnotes for more details.

1

Create an Account / Sign In

Just like our mobile app, creating an account is free. Your account will give you access to a dashboard where you can begin to track your policies growth; invite grandparents or other friends and family to contribute to the savings plan; where you manage your cash value; and where you can see peace of mind.

2

Payment & Completion

To complete your purchase there are two easy ways to pay: 1) credit or debit card or 2) bill pay via your online bank.

3

Medical History Questionnaire

The Medical History Questionnaire only takes a few minutes to compelte. It allows us to ensure that when your child applies for more coverage, they don't have to go through cumbersome underwritting procedures as an adult.

4

Underwritting

Northwestern Mutual places more policyholders in "Best Class" than any other insurer. Your child will have several opportunities as an adult to increase their dealth benefit without having to undergo any cumbersome underwriting tests as an adult or being at risk of being denied insurance for medical reasons that surfaced during the childhood.

Confirmation
