Why are prices different between boys and girls?

As a whole men have lower life expectency than women. Men generally tend to have a much riskier lifestyle than women. All you have to do is look at automobile accident statistics and you will see that males have a much higher accident and fatality rate than women. Young males especially also seem to engage on average in much riskier activities than women. Activities such as contact sports, many forms of racing activities such as auto and motorcycling racing, and other similar activities often result in higher mortality rates for men than women who tend on average to engage in less dangerous hobbies and sporting activities. Males also tend to take more risks in their activities than women do which results in a higher rate of both injury and premature death. Lastly, men tend to lead less healthier lifestyles and be employeed in riskier occupations.

Just like our mobile app, creating an account is free. Your account will give you access to a dashboard where you can begin to track your policies growth; where grandparents or other friends and family can contribute; where you manage your cash value; and where you can see peace of mind.

To complete your purchase there are two easy ways to pay: 1) credit or debit card or 2) bill pay via your online bank.

By far the most involved process is completign the Medical History Questionnaire. This document allows us to ensure that when the applies for more coverage, they don't have to go through complicated underwritting processes.
